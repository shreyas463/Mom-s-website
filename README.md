# Dr. Rashmi Chaudhary

Official website of **Dr. Rashmi Chaudhary**, Senior Consultant Obstetrician &
Gynaecologist at Cloudnine Hospitals, Bangalore.

🌐 **Live site:** https://rashmichaudhary.netlify.app/

## About Dr. Chaudhary

Dr. Rashmi Chaudhary is a Senior Consultant Obstetrician & Gynaecologist with
over **26 years of experience**, and has cared for families at Cloudnine
Hospitals, Bangalore for more than six years. She specialises in high-risk
pregnancy, fetal medicine, minimally invasive (laparoscopic) gynaecological
surgery, and fertility care.

She consults in English, Hindi, Bengali, Marathi and Kannada, and is known for
explaining things clearly and patiently so that every patient feels informed and
well cared for.

The website brings together her background, areas of expertise, patient ratings,
educational videos, clinic locations, and an easy way to request an appointment.

## How to book an appointment

Requesting a consultation takes about a minute:

1. Open the website and click **Book a Consultation**.
2. Enter your name, phone number and preferred date.
3. Choose a **virtual (online video)** consultation or an **in-person** visit at
   Cloudnine (Bellandur or Sarjapur Road).
4. Add the reason for your visit if you'd like, then submit.
5. Dr. Chaudhary's team receives your request and calls you back to confirm the
   date and time.

Prefer to speak to someone right away? You can also tap **Chat on WhatsApp** or
**Call** on the site to reach the team directly.

> For a medical emergency, please contact your nearest hospital instead of using
> the appointment form.

## Your privacy & security

- The site runs over a secure, encrypted (HTTPS) connection.
- The details you enter are used only to arrange your appointment and are sent
  straight to Dr. Chaudhary's team — nothing is stored on the website.
- No ads, no tracking and no cookies.
